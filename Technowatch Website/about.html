ABOUT
US

Technowatch is a student-driven tech organization from TUP-Visayas, powered by passionate Computer Engineering Technology students.

We are dedicated to transforming ideas into impactful hardware and software solutions that resonate with our community and drive technological advancement at TUPV.

Since 2023

Our Community

Technowatch was founded by a group of passionate students who saw a need for a collaborative space dedicated to hands-on technology. We started with just a few microcontrollers and one big idea: to create a community where any student, regardless of skill level, could come to learn, build, and innovate.

From our first simple power supply to today's complex AI models, our club has grown into a thriving hub for TUP-V's best designers, developers, and engineersâ€”all driven by a shared curiosity and a passion for building what's next.
