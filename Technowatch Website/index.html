About Us

Technowatch is a student-driven tech organization from TUP-Visayas, powered by passionate Computer Engineering Technology students. We explore innovation, develop projects, and build a community where learning and creativity thrive.

Meet the Team

What We Do

We engage in hands-on tech activities—robotics, coding, electronics, research, digital design, and system development—while helping students sharpen real-world skills that go beyond the classroom.

Our Purpose

To inspire future innovators, promote technical excellence, and create a supportive space where every member can grow, collaborate, and lead.
